Computer Scientist

I am a Software Developer/Engineer who is passionate about coding. I am a graduate of Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Visayas State University. I enjoy tinkering and problem-solving.

I have made a personal blog website for fun. Most of my blog posts are my notes that I converted into tutorials. "You learn more when you teach it", they said, and I can assure you that this works. It also helps me offload some of the technical stuff I have learned to a platform where I can easily retrieve them. I am currently adding more features to the blog like a twitter-like feed for my thoughts that I want to share. I am also developing an Android app as a utility app for me to post without going directly to the website.

On some weekends, I have worked as a programming tutor. The students who requested the tutorial used Java in their class. I am not proficient in Java but have experienced coding with it during my college years. So whenever I get a request for a tutorial, I study the language again, then focus on the topics where my student is currently on. This has worked for me, and my student. The student has learned programming concepts like OOP where the student previously struggled during their class at school. The tutorials also helped me learn the language more and improve my skills as a programmer overall.

I have worked as a Software developer at the Local Government Unit of Tunga, and have made their official website tunga.gov.ph. I have also made an Inventory Management System for their office supplies and medicines, and a Document Management System for the Mayor's Office. Unfortunately, I wasn't able to fully implement those two Systems due to reasons I shouldn't disclose.

You can check out a few of my coding projects at my Github account.
